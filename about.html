About the Author

John Oganda

Hello! I'm John Oganda, a student of Computer Science at Chuka University who is passionate about life skills, personal development and sharing knowledge that inspires change. I created this website as a resource to help others grow and succeed using simple, practical principles.

Most of the information here comes from books, mentors, personal research and lived experience. I believe success is not just for the few- Its for those who are ready to grow and lead with purpose.

Keep learning. Keep growing. Keep sharing.

Where the Information Comes From

The ideas and advice shared on this site are based on:

  • Atomic Habitsby James Clear
  • The 7 Habits of Highly Effective Peopleby Stephen R. Covey
  • Deep Workby Cal Newport
  • Think and Grow Richby Napoleon Hill
  • Advice from mentors, feedbacks from readers and real-life experience

My Vision

My goal is to make life advice accessible and useful for fellow students, dreamers and anyone looking to improve themselves. Every keyy shared here is a step toward your future success
