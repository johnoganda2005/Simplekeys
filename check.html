Checklist for Personal Growth

  • ✅ Wake up before 6:30 AM and avoid your phone for the first hour
  • ✅ Drink a full glass of water and stretch or exercise
  • ✅ Write down your top 3 goals for the day
  • ✅ Read or listen to 10+ minutes of something educational
  • ✅ Take at least one step toward your long-term goal
  • ✅ Avoid gossip, distractions, or time-wasting habits
  • ✅ Reflect on your actions in the evening and journal your progress
  • ✅ Sleep before 10:30 PM and prepare for the next day
